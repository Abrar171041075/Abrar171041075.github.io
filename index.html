Teaching Philosophy

Serving in academia as a university teacher with students from different countries, backgrounds, and cultures, I maintain a task-based approach using the PPP (Present-Practice-Production) teaching method with a lead-in activity in my sessions. My class is not only for delivering lectures; rather, it is about sharing information and experiences and presenting technological paradigms to the students. I prefer both auditory and visual techniques to deliver lectures and to understand the needs analysis of my students.

The most important part is that whatever they are learning in class, they can relate to the real world through necessary real-life examples. I maintain this strategy strictly because if they understand the outcomes of the topics, it makes the subject and concept more interesting rather than just memorizing them.
